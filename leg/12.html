📅 Day by day

Tue Aug 4
Morskie Oko hike
Iconic Tatra alpine lake. Frequent minibus/bus from Zakopane to Palenica Białczańska (~40 min), or a taxi. 9 km uphill walk on a paved road (2h) to the lake. Horse-cart shortcut available. Lunch at Schronisko PTTK Morskie Oko (mountain refuge).
Wed Aug 5
Kasprowy Wierch cable car + ridge walk
Cable car from Kuźnice up to 1985m (book online — fixed time slots). Ridge walk along the Polish-Slovak border. Easier descent option via the Hala Gąsienicowa trail.
Thu Aug 6
Gubałówka + Krupówki day
Funicular up Gubałówka — panoramic views, summer alpine slides, food stalls. Afternoon: stroll Krupówki shopping street, Tatra Museum, oscypek cheese tasting. Spa hour at Aries.
Fri Aug 7
Train Zakopane → Warszawa
Pack out, taxi to Zakopane station. PKP Intercity direct Zakopane → Warszawa, ~5h30–7h (the seasonal "Tatry"/"Giewont" trains run straight through). Book ahead — August is peak and these sell out. Taxi from Warszawa to Buchnik (Leg 13) for the Aug 8 party. Book: koleo.pl or intercity.pl.

🏔️ Tatra hikes & attractions

💎

Morskie Oko

"Eye of the Sea" — the most famous alpine lake in Poland. Photo-perfect. The walk is on a road, doable with Lily.

Map
🚠

Kasprowy Wierch cable car

Up to 1985m in 20 min. Book timed slots online to avoid queues.

Tickets Map
🎢

Gubałówka funicular

From the top of Krupówki. Easy summit, summer alpine slides, kid-friendly views.

Tickets Map
🥾

Dolina Kościeliska

Family-friendly valley hike — meadows, caves, mountain stream. ~3h round-trip, almost flat. Best easy hike with kids.

Map
🎶

Krupówki street

Pedestrian main drag — restaurants, oscypek vendors, highlander folk music, souvenir shops.

Map
🏛️

Tatrzańskie Muzeum (Tatra Museum)

Goral (highlander) culture + natural history. Quick & cheap, good rainy-day option.

Site Map
⛪

Wooden Chapel of Jaszczurówka

Zakopane-style wooden chapel on the way to Morskie Oko. Free, quick photo stop.

Map
♨️

Termy Bukovina (or Chochołowskie)

Outdoor thermal pools with mountain views. Great rainy-day or sore-legs evening.
