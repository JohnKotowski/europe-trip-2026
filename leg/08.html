📅 Day by day

Thu Jul 30
Sintra big day
Early bus/Uber to Sintra (30 min, beat the crowds). Pena Palace at opening (9:30am), then Quinta da Regaleira (initiation well, magical gardens). Lunch in Sintra centro. Castelo dos Mouros if energy. Back to Cascais for evening swim.
Fri Jul 31
Cabo da Roca + wild beaches
Drive (or 403 bus) to Cabo da Roca — westernmost point of continental Europe. Hike down to Praia da Ursa (steep, dramatic) OR easier swim at Praia do Guincho (windy, surfy). Sunset at Boca do Inferno on the way home.
Sat Aug 1
Cascais morning → Lisbon → ORY
Slow morning at Praia da Rainha (in town). Lunch, pack, train to Lisbon airport (~1h). Flight to Paris ORY — see Leg 9.

🏰 Sintra UNESCO palaces

🎨

Palácio Nacional da Pena

The candy-colored Romanticist palace on top of the Sintra hills. Book first time slot, skip the queue.

Tickets Map
🌀

Quinta da Regaleira

Mystical mansion with the spiral initiation well (kid magnet). Allow 2h. Buy timed ticket online.

Tickets Map
🏯

Castelo dos Mouros

9th-c. Moorish hilltop castle. Climb the ramparts. Combine with Pena Palace ticket.

Tickets Map
🏛️

Palácio Nacional de Sintra

In Sintra town center — twin conical chimneys. Older + quieter than Pena. Worth a quick visit.

Tickets Map
🌿

Parque e Palácio de Monserrate

Underrated palace + exotic gardens. Quieter than the others. Great if you have a 4th Sintra day.

Tickets

🌊 Cabo da Roca & beaches

🗼

Cabo da Roca

Westernmost point of continental Europe. Cliff lighthouse + Camões quote. Windy, dramatic.

Map
🦅

Praia da Ursa

Wild, hidden beach reached via a steep cliff trail. No facilities. For the adventurous — Joshua & Mark will love it.

Map
🏄

Praia do Guincho

Big Atlantic surf beach. Windy but spectacular. Surf schools + a few beach restaurants.

Map
🌊

Boca do Inferno

"Hell's mouth" — dramatic sea-carved chasm 10 min west of Cascais. Free, quick stop, sunset spot.

Map
👑

Praia da Rainha (Cascais centre)

Tiny gem beach steps from Cascais old town. Easy swim during downtime.

Map

🖼️ Museums in Cascais

🎭

Casa das Histórias Paula Rego

Striking rust-red pyramid building. Major Portuguese contemporary artist. Quick + free 1st Sunday.

Site Map
🏛️

Museu Condes de Castro Guimarães

Quirky 19th-c. mansion on the seafront. Park around it is lovely.
