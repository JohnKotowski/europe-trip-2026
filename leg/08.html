📅 Day by day

Tue Jul 28 (eve)
Arrive Lisbon
In from the Lagos train (Leg 5), into central Lisbon. Drop bags in Baixa, then wander up to the castle viewpoints, ride Tram 28, dinner in Alfama or a Time Out Market grazing run.
Wed Jul 29
Sintra day trip (or Lisbon city day)
The one full day — pick your headline. Sintra: ~40-min train from Rossio (go early to beat crowds), Pena Palace at opening + Quinta da Regaleira (initiation well, magical gardens), lunch in Sintra centro, back to Lisbon for the evening. Or stay in town: Belém (Jerónimos, pastéis de Belém, the tower) by tram/train + Alfama and the castle.
Thu Jul 30
Lisbon morning → airport
Slow morning in Baixa — a last pastel de nata. Pack, then metro Red line direct to Aeroporto (~20 min) — aim to be at LIS by ~12:30 PM for the 2:30 PM Lufthansa to Kraków via Munich (Leg 11).

Note: 2 nights here (Cascais leg was 3) — one full day. Sintra is the obvious day trip; if you'd rather do both Sintra and Lisbon properly, this leg needs a 3rd night.

🚇 Getting around — no car

  • In Lisbon: walkable Baixa/Alfama + Metro, trams (28/15), and Bolt/Uber. Castelo de São Jorge is a short uphill walk or Tram 28.
  • Sintra: CP train from Rossio station (~40 min, frequent). Then bus #434 loops the palaces.
  • Belém: Tram 15 or a short train from Cais do Sodré.
  • Cascais / Cabo da Roca (optional): CP coastal train from Cais do Sodré to Cascais (~40 min), then Scotturb #403 to Cabo da Roca.
  • LIS airport on departure day: Metro Red line direct to Aeroporto (~20 min from Baixa).

🏰 Sintra UNESCO palaces

🎨

Palácio Nacional da Pena

The candy-colored Romanticist palace on top of the Sintra hills. Book first time slot, skip the queue.

Tickets Map
🌀

Quinta da Regaleira

Mystical mansion with the spiral initiation well (kid magnet). Allow 2h. Buy timed ticket online.

Tickets Map
🏯

Castelo dos Mouros

9th-c. Moorish hilltop castle. Climb the ramparts. Combine with Pena Palace ticket.

Tickets Map
🏛️

Palácio Nacional de Sintra

In Sintra town center — twin conical chimneys. Older + quieter than Pena. Worth a quick visit.

Tickets Map
🌿

Parque e Palácio de Monserrate

Underrated palace + exotic gardens. Quieter than the others. Great if you have a 4th Sintra day.
