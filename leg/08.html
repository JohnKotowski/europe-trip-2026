📅 Day by day

Tue Jul 28 (eve)
Arrive Cascais
In from the Lagos train ~3 PM (Leg 5). Settle in, evening swim at Praia da Rainha, dinner in town.
Wed Jul 29
Sintra big day (or Cabo da Roca)
The one full day — pick your headline. Sintra: early bus/Uber (30 min, beat the crowds), Pena Palace at opening (9:30am) + Quinta da Regaleira (initiation well, magical gardens), lunch in Sintra centro. Or coast: Scotturb bus #403 to Cabo da Roca (westernmost cliffs) + a swim at Praia do Guincho (#405). Evening: Boca do Inferno walk + sunset in Cascais.
Thu Jul 30
Cascais morning → Lisbon airport
Slow morning at Praia da Rainha. Lunch, pack. Train Cascais → Cais do Sodré, metro to Aeroporto — aim to be at LIS by ~12:30 PM for the 2:30 PM Lufthansa to Kraków via Munich (Leg 11).

Note: trimmed to 2 nights (was 3) — one fewer full day than the old plan. If you want both Sintra and a full Cabo da Roca day, this leg needs a 3rd night back.

🚌 Getting around — no car

  • Sintra: CP train Cascais → Lisbon → Sintra (~1h, change at Cais do Sodré or Rossio) OR direct Scotturb bus #417 from Cascais (40 min). Then bus #434 loops the palaces.
  • Cabo da Roca / Praia da Ursa: Scotturb bus #403 from Cascais station (Cascais ↔ Cabo da Roca ↔ Sintra loop)
  • Praia do Guincho: Scotturb bus #405
  • Boca do Inferno + Cascais beaches: walking
  • LIS airport on departure day: CP train Cascais → Cais do Sodré → metro Red line direct to Aeroporto

🏰 Sintra UNESCO palaces

🎨

Palácio Nacional da Pena

The candy-colored Romanticist palace on top of the Sintra hills. Book first time slot, skip the queue.

Tickets Map
🌀

Quinta da Regaleira

Mystical mansion with the spiral initiation well (kid magnet). Allow 2h. Buy timed ticket online.

Tickets Map
🏯

Castelo dos Mouros

9th-c. Moorish hilltop castle. Climb the ramparts. Combine with Pena Palace ticket.

Tickets Map
🏛️

Palácio Nacional de Sintra

In Sintra town center — twin conical chimneys. Older + quieter than Pena. Worth a quick visit.

Tickets Map
🌿

Parque e Palácio de Monserrate

Underrated palace + exotic gardens. Quieter than the others. Great if you have a 4th Sintra day.

Tickets

🌊 Cabo da Roca & beaches

🗼

Cabo da Roca

Westernmost point of continental Europe. Cliff lighthouse + Camões quote. Windy, dramatic.

Map
🦅

Praia da Ursa

Wild, hidden beach reached via a steep cliff trail. No facilities. For the adventurous — Joshua & Mark will love it.

Map
🏄

Praia do Guincho

Big Atlantic surf beach. Windy but spectacular. Surf schools + a few beach restaurants.

Map
🌊

Boca do Inferno

"Hell's mouth" — dramatic sea-carved chasm 10 min west of Cascais. Free, quick stop, sunset spot.

Map
👑

Praia da Rainha (Cascais centre)

Tiny gem beach steps from Cascais old town. Easy swim during downtime.

Map
