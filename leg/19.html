🎯 Top things in Kraków

🏰

Wawel Castle + Cathedral

Royal seat for 500 years. State Rooms, Cathedral, Crown Treasury. Allow 3h. Buy tickets online — they sell timed slots and sell out in summer.

Tickets Map
⛪

Rynek Główny + St Mary's Basilica

Europe's largest medieval square. St Mary's altarpiece by Veit Stoss is a must — climb the tower if Joshua's up for it. Underground Museum (Podziemia Rynku) is great for kids.

Map
🕯️

Kazimierz (Jewish Quarter)

Synagogues, atmospheric streets, the food/bar scene of Plac Nowy. Pre-WWII Jewish heart of Kraków. Walk over the Father Bernatek footbridge to Podgórze for Schindler's Factory.

Map
🏭

Schindler's Factory Museum

Excellent WWII / Holocaust history exhibit in Oskar Schindler's actual enamelware factory. Heavy — gauge for kids 13/14 OK, may be heavy for Lily.

Tickets Map
⛏️

Wieliczka Salt Mine (day trip)

UNESCO underground cathedrals carved into rock salt. 13 km outside Kraków. 3h guided tour. Memorable for kids. Book the English-language slot.

Tickets Map
🥨

Obwarzanek krakowski (street pretzels)

The chewy ring bread sold from blue carts all over Old Town. ~3 PLN each. Standard Kraków snack.

🍽️ Eat & drink

  • Pod Aniołami · classic Polish in 13th-c. cellars off Grodzka — great first dinner
  • Miod Malina · cozy traditional Polish on Grodzka, beloved by tourists for good reason
  • Pierogarnia Krakowiacy · cheap, perfect pierogi sampler — Sławkowska street
  • Plac Nowy zapiekankas · stalls open till late, Kazimierz street food classic
  • Klezmer-Hois · Jewish-Polish in Kazimierz with live klezmer music some nights
  • Wesele · upscale on Main Square if you want a celebration dinner

ℹ️ Notes

  • Old Town is fully walkable — leave the rental at a city garage (e.g., Parking Plac Świętego Ducha or hotel parking) for the 2-night stay
