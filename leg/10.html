Pivot stretch between Portugal and the Poland leg · 3 days to refresh, restock, decompress

Free stay · Sister's place 2ter impasse Marie Luise, 78600 Maisons-Laffitte · RER A line · same as the first Paris stop. Map

From ORY (Wed Jul 29 arrival): taxi ~50-60 min cross-Paris (~€70-90) OR OrlyVal+RER B+RER A ~90 min. Taxi recommended with luggage.

📅 Day by day

Wed Jul 29
Land Paris from Lisbon · settle in
Transavia lands ORY 1:05 PM. RER B / taxi to sister's. Easy afternoon — kids decompress after Portugal. Casual dinner nearby.
Thu Jul 30
Paris with no agenda
Pick whichever museum or neighbourhood didn't fit the first stop. Louvre if the kids are up for it (early time slot, hit just the highlights). Or a relaxed day at Versailles or Vincennes.
Fri Jul 31
🎂 Karine's birthday — "Karine's Day"
Morning: let Karine sleep in. Coffee + pastries from a Maisons-Laffitte boulangerie.
Midday → afternoon: day-trip to Giverny (Monet's Garden). ~1h via SNCF Paris Saint-Lazare → Vernon-Giverny then 15-min shuttle bus. Famous water-lily pond + pink-and-green Monet house. Lunch at Hôtel Baudy (the Impressionists' old tavern, still serving). Quiet, romantic.
Return: back to Maisons-Laffitte by ~5–6 PM, brief reset.
🍽 Birthday dinner: Bouillon Julien (16 rue du Faubourg Saint-Denis, 10e) — stunning Belle Époque interior, generous Paris classics, kid-friendly, ~€35pp. Book the table NOW for July 31 (reservations open ~2 months ahead). Include Pat + her kids if she's around — John will invite verbally.
🍰 Cake: pre-order a French gâteau from Stohrer (oldest pâtisserie in Paris, 1730) or Sébastien Gaudard. Drop with the restaurant in advance.
Sat Aug 1
Third Paris day (bonus from rebooked flight)
Open day in/around Paris. Earlier plan had this as a flight day — now you have one more full Paris day before the Sunday afternoon flight. Use it for whatever didn't fit (Louvre, Musée d'Orsay, picnic) or take it easy. Pack tonight for Poland.
Sun Aug 2
Late morning → CDG → Kraków
Slow morning at sister's, brunch, then taxi/RER ~11 AM to CDG. easyJet 1:20 PM nonstop to Kraków, lands 3:35 PM. See Leg 11 for the flight + onward Kraków 1-night stay (Leg 19).
