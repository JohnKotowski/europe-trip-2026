📅 Day by day

Sun Aug 9
Drive Warsaw → Bogaczewo · settle in
~3h drive NW via S7. Provision groceries en route in Mława or Olsztynek. Check in at Plaża Resort, lake swim from the resort beach, group dinner at Restauracja Plaża.
Mon Aug 10
Sailing day on Lake Narie
Half-day sail/dinghy rental at the Bogaczewo harbor (Morąg Sailing Club "Keja" is here, plus mJacht yacht charter). Quiet zone = motorless, peaceful sailing. PM: SUP / kayak on the lake, jetty cannonballs.
Tue Aug 11
Ostróda–Elbląg Canal cruise
~50-min drive S to Ostróda. One of Europe's most unusual waterways — boats are hauled overland on rail carts to cross level differences (100m+). Half-day cruise, lunch in Ostróda. Big-kid magnet.
Wed Aug 12
Morąg town + lake afternoon
Morning: Morąg (10 min drive) — Gothic town hall (1444), Dohna Palace / Museum of Warmia & Mazury, Teutonic castle ruins. Lunch in town. PM: chill at the resort — spa hour, bowling, fishing or another swim. Big group BBQ in the evening.
Thu Aug 13
Pack out → Buchnik
Late breakfast, group photos, ~3h drive back to Wieslawa's parents. See Leg 15.
