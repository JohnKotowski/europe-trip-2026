🎯 Attractions in Mazury

🐺

Wolfsschanze (Wolf's Lair)

Hitler's WWII Eastern Front HQ. Massive concrete bunker ruins in a forest. Audio guide essential. 2-3h visit.

Site Map

Mikołajki marina

"Sailing capital of Poland". Boat rental, restaurants, lake-front strolling. Best base for a day's exploration.

19th-c. Prussian star fort with ramparts to explore. Kids' climbing playground.
