Sun Jul 19 – Wed Jul 22 · 3 nights

Paris @ sister's Free

Recover from overnight · acclimatize · short Paris sampler before flying to Portugal

🏠 Stay

Free stay · Sister's place 2ter impasse Marie Luise, 78600 Maisons-Laffitte · RER A line, ~30 min to central Paris (Étoile). Map

From CDG: ~30 min taxi/Uber (~€60-80) — easiest with luggage. RER B + RER A change via Châtelet is ~75 min.

📅 Day by day

Sun Jul 19
Arrival & nap day
Land 6am, taxi/RER to sister's, brunch. Big nap. Light evening walk — Seine + Tuileries if energy allows. Early dinner, early bed.
Mon Jul 20
Paris hits with the kids
Eiffel Tower (book online), picnic at Champ-de-Mars, Trocadéro views. Afternoon: Sainte-Chapelle (kid-friendly stained glass, 20 min visit), Notre-Dame exterior (reopened 2024). Crêpes for dinner.
Tue Jul 21
Extra Paris day
Bonus day from the +1 shift. Musée d'Orsay or a Seine boat, Jardin du Luxembourg, Le Marais wander + falafel on Rue des Rosiers. Or a half-day Versailles run if the kids are up for it.
Wed Jul 22
Slow morning → CDG
Boulangerie breakfast, last stroll. Pack and head to CDG by ~1pm for the 4:10pm flight to Faro via Amsterdam (Leg 3).

Eiffel Tower

🏛️

Sainte-Chapelle

15-min stained-glass spectacle on Île de la Cité. Kids gawk, adults gawk too. Skip-the-line ticket worth it.

Tickets Map
⛪

Notre-Dame de Paris

Reopened Dec 2024 after fire restoration. Free entry, queues form before 10am. Exterior alone is impressive.

Site Map
🖼️

Musée d'Orsay

More kid-friendly than the Louvre (smaller, Impressionist hits). 2-3h. Wed late nights to 9:45pm.

Tickets Map

Jardin du Luxembourg

Best park for kids — sailboat pond, playground (small fee), pony rides. Great picnic spot.
