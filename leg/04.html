📅 Day by day

Wed Jul 22
Beach day — Praia Dona Ana
Easy first day. Iconic golden cliffs, lifeguarded, beach bar. Lunch at Mar d'Estórias or grab takeaway. Pool/nap PM.
Thu Jul 23
Ponta da Piedade kayak tour
Morning sea-kayak through the famous limestone arches and grottos. 2.5h. Book ahead — sells out in summer. Afternoon swim at Praia do Camilo (200 steps down, worth it).
Fri Jul 24
Sagres + Cabo de São Vicente day trip
Drive 45 min to Sagres. Fortress, cliffs, surf school for the boys (optional). Sunset at Cabo de São Vicente — the SW corner of Europe.
Sat Jul 25
Benagil cave + Praia da Marinha
Drive 30 min east to Carvoeiro. Boat tour to Benagil sea cave (Instagram famous — but real). Lunch + swim at Praia da Marinha — repeatedly voted best beach in Europe.
Sun Jul 26
Pack out → train
Slow morning. Drop rental at Lagos station. 11am-ish train to Lisbon onward to Setúbal. See Leg 5.

🏖️ Beaches

🌅

Praia Dona Ana

Cliff-flanked cove, golden cliffs. 10 min from Lagos. Lifeguard + beach bar.

Map
🪜

Praia do Camilo

200 wooden steps down to a tiny twin-cove gem. Cliff-walk views from above.

Map
⛵

Praia da Marinha

30 min drive. Often voted #1 beach in Europe. Clear water, dramatic rocks. Combine with Benagil.

Map
🌴

Praia da Luz

Family-friendly beach in nearby Luz village. Calm, sandy, restaurants on the boardwalk.
