🏨 Quinta do Lago · Jul 22–24 Locked

Lakeside Country Club · 2BR apartment · Hotels.com #72075423945794

Wed Jul 22 — arrival night
Land Faro → Quinta do Lago
Flight lands FAO 9:20 PM. Pre-book a transfer or grab a Bolt (~15–20 min, ~€25) to the apartment. Keep it simple — settle in, light supper, sleep.
Thu Jul 23
Ria Formosa & an easy first beach day
Morning: slow start, supermarket run, then head to the Quinta do Lago boardwalk — the long wooden footbridge over the Ria Formosa lagoon to Praia do Ancão (long calm sandy beach, lifeguarded).
Getting there (car-free): the boardwalk is at the south end of the resort by the Quinta do Lago beach car park. Walk ~1.5–2.5 km down Avenida André Jordan toward the beach (~20–30 min, flat); or Bolt/taxi ~5 min to the "Quinta do Lago praia / beach" car park (boardwalk right there); or rent bikes — QdL is very bike-friendly with flat dedicated paths. Easiest: Bolt down in the morning, walk the boardwalk, stroll or Bolt back.
Lunch: the famous Gigi's beach shack (book ahead) or a café by the boardwalk. Afternoon: swim + lagoon birdlife; optional kayak/SUP rental on the lagoon. Evening: stroll the QdL square, dinner out. Optional: 15-min taxi to Faro old town (Cidade Velha) — cathedral, walls, bone chapel — if you want a town evening.

🏖️ Lagos / Praia da Luz · Jul 24–28 Locked

Apartment República, Porto de Mós · Airbnb conf HMWEEWHY95 · check-in after 4 PM

Fri Jul 24
West to Luz → first cliff beach
Morning: check out QdL by 11 AM, transfer west (train Faro→Lagos ~1h40 with a change at Tunes, or a private transfer ~1h door-to-door). Check in after 4 PM at Porto de Mós. Evening: ease in at Praia do Porto de Mós (right below the apartment) or the calm Praia da Luz village beach — sunset + dinner on the Luz seafront.
Sat Jul 25
Ponta da Piedade — kayak the arches
Morning: sea-kayak or SUP tour through the Ponta da Piedade grottos (2–2.5h, ~€30 pp) — the iconic golden arches & sea caves. Book 1–2 weeks ahead, morning slot for calm water. Afternoon: 200 steps down to Praia do Camilo (tiny twin cove) or the bigger Praia Dona Ana. Evening: wander Lagos old town — Igreja de Santo António, dinner at A Forja or Casinha do Petisco.
Sun Jul 26
Sagres + Cabo de São Vicente
The wild south-west tip. Option A: organized half/full-day tour with pickup (~€55–65 pp). Option B (car-free DIY): Vamus bus #14 Lagos → Sagres (~1h, €4). See the Fortaleza de Sagres cliff fort and Cabo de São Vicente lighthouse — Europe's south-westernmost point, best at sunset (pack windbreakers + snacks). Beach option en route: Praia do Beliche / Praia da Mareta.
Mon Jul 27
Benagil cave + Praia da Marinha — boat day
Full- or half-day boat tour from Lagos marina (10-min walk from town) sailing east along the cliffs into the Benagil sea cave (the skylight grotto) with a swim/lunch stop near Praia da Marinha (~€55–75 pp). Kid bonus: many operators add a dolphin-watching loop. Easy evening back in Luz — pack tonight.

🏛️ Lisbon · Jul 28–30 Open

Front-runner: Lisbon Serviced Apartments · Baixa Castelo (not booked yet)

Tue Jul 28
Train to Lisbon → first evening in town
Morning: check out of Luz by 10 AM, Bolt to Lagos station, train Lagos → Lisbon (~3h30, one change at Tunes). Arrive early afternoon, metro/taxi to Baixa. Evening: drop bags, walk up to Castelo de São Jorge viewpoints, ride Tram 28, dinner in Alfama (fado optional) or graze the Time Out Market.
Wed Jul 29
Sintra day trip (or a full Lisbon city day)
Sintra: ~40-min train from Rossio — go early. Pena Palace at opening (pre-book timed ticket) + Quinta da Regaleira (the spiral initiation well — kid magnet); lunch in Sintra centro; back to Lisbon for the evening. Or stay in the city: Belém (Jerónimos Monastery, Belém Tower, the original pastéis de Belém) by tram 15, plus Praça do Comércio & the Baixa streets.
Thu Jul 30
Lisbon morning → fly to Kraków
Slow morning in Baixa — one last pastel de nata. Pack and take the Metro red line direct to the airport (~20 min). Be at LIS by ~12:30 PM for the 2:30 PM Lufthansa to Kraków via Munich (flight detail). Portugal done — onto Poland.
