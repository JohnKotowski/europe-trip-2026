📅 Day by day

Mon Jul 27
Galapinhos beach + Arrábida hike
Drive into the Arrábida park. Park at Galapinhos (shuttle in peak summer — check). Hidden cove, turquoise water, dramatic cliffs. Quick afternoon hike on a marked trail.
Tue Jul 28
Sado dolphin tour + Setúbal market
Morning: 3h boat trip to spot the resident bottlenose pod in the Sado estuary. Lunch at Setúbal Mercado do Livramento (Portugal's most beautiful fish market). PM: pool / Convento da Arrábida visit.
Wed Jul 29
Sesimbra detour → train to Cascais
Morning swim at Praia do Portinho da Arrábida. Late check-out, drop rental in Setúbal, train to Cascais. See Leg 7.

🏖️ Beaches in Arrábida

🌊

Praia dos Galapinhos

Repeatedly voted best beach in Europe. White sand, glass-clear water, cliff backdrop. Peak summer requires shuttle from Portinho.

Map
⛵

Praia do Portinho da Arrábida

Tiny fishing village + sheltered cove. Beach restaurants for grilled fish lunch.

Map
🏞️

Praia dos Coelhos

15-min walk down a wooded path from Galapinhos. Even more secluded.

🎯 Attractions & activities

🐬

Sado Estuary Dolphin Tour

3h sail. Resident pod of ~30 bottlenose. Vertigem Azul + Mar Ilimitado are reputable operators.

Vertigem Azul Map
⛪

Convento da Arrábida

16th-c. monastery hung on a forested slope above the bay. Guided visits only — book ahead.

Map
🐟

Mercado do Livramento (Setúbal)

Voted best fish market in the world by USA Today. Stunning azulejo tile murals. Go early morning.

Map
🏰

Castelo de São Filipe

16th-c. star fort above Setúbal. Sweeping bay views, tile-clad chapel.

Map
🍷

José Maria da Fonseca winery (Azeitão)

Oldest table-wine producer in Portugal (1834). Tour + tasting — moscatel a highlight. 20 min from Setúbal.

Site Map
🏰

Sesimbra Castle

Moorish castle ruins above Sesimbra. Quick stop on the way back from beaches.

Map
